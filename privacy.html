Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user information for the HeyCaddyAI mobile application and Wear OS companion app.

1. Information Collection and Use

Location Data:
To provide accurate golf course distances and shot dispersion logic, our app requires access to your device's Precise Location. This data is processed ephemerally to calculate distances and is not stored permanently on external servers.

Microphone / Voice Data:
The app requests microphone access solely to capture your voice commands. Audio is processed ephemerally. The resulting text transcript is transmitted to Google's Gemini AI API for processing. Raw audio files are never stored or retained by HeyCaddyAI.

App Performance & Diagnostics:
To ensure the "Pocket-Mode" service and Wear OS integration run smoothly, the app may collect diagnostic information and crash logs (e.g., through Android Vitals or Firebase). This data is used solely for analytics to improve app stability and does not identify you personally.

Device Identifiers:
The app may use standard device identifiers (such as Firebase Installation IDs or Advertising IDs) provided by Google Play Services to facilitate app functionality, analytics, and secure communication between your phone and Wear OS watch.

Bluetooth / Wear OS:
The app uses Bluetooth to synchronize data between your phone and Wear OS device. This communication occurs strictly between your personal devices.

Shot Statistics:
Club distances and round statistics are stored locally on your device. You can delete all stored data at any time via Settings or by uninstalling the app.

2. Data Security & Encryption

Encryption in Transit:
All data transmitted between the HeyCaddyAI app and third-party services (such as Google Gemini or mapping APIs) is encrypted in transit using modern HTTPS (TLS) protocols to protect your information from unauthorized access during transmission.

3. Third-Party Services

HeyCaddyAI integrates the following services, governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API — AI response processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services — Location and Wear OS data layer (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — Golf course map data.

4. Data Sharing & Deletion

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Because HeyCaddyAI does not require a user account, all performance data is stored locally. You may delete your data by:

  • Clearing the app cache/data in Android Settings.
  • Uninstalling the application.
